Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, also known as the "Iron Man of India," was a courageous and determined freedom fighter who played a crucial role in uniting the princely states after India's independence.

What I admire most about him is his strong leadership, practical thinking, and unwavering dedication to national unity. His calm yet firm approach in difficult times teaches us the importance of patience and resolve.

He led by example and inspired millions with his selfless service to the nation. His legacy lives on in the hearts of every Indian.
